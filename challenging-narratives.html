Why Challenging Narratives Matters

By Emmanuel Beneth | July 2025

Too often, societies accept surface explanations and inherited ideas without interrogation. But challenging narratives is where real transformation begins. From governance failures to inequitable policies, it's our responsibility to question the stories we’ve been handed.

This reflection explores why critical thinking isn’t just a personal trait but a civic duty — essential for building fairer systems and more resilient communities across Africa and the Global South.

Key points:

  • How stale thinking perpetuates inequality
  • The power of reframing problems beyond economic lenses
  • Tools to cultivate a questioning mind in policy spaces

If we fail to question, we fail to advance. Let’s rethink together.
